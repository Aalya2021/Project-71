Admission

  • Process for admission

    Process Of Admission. Admission in Scottish High International School is purely based on seat availability. However, siblings of existing children would be given priority. Applicants will be invited via email for an Online Document Verification process (depending on seat availability). This will be facilitated over any video calling platform like Zoom/Google Hangout/Skype etc. Registration is not a guarantee for admission. Given the fact that the seats are limited, the school shall endeavour to maintain a fair approach towards all candidates and proceed on the basis of the required criteria being met. Registration Charges: Rs. 2000/- (Both Online and Offline). Online Registration Process: Fill up the registration form on the school website, https://reg.scottishigh.com/registration.aspx . Pay the registration fee online. You will receive confirmation email and SMS with Online payment Id and Provisional Registration Number. Upload the required documents using Online payment Id and Provisional Registration Number. Documents to be Uploaded: Birth certificate of the student. Photograph of the student Photograph of Parents Please upload passport size photos only. System will accept only png/jpg files not more than 100 kb size in case of photograph and not more than 300 kb size in case of Birth Certificate. For mid-term admissions please email admission dept on – schoolcoord@scottishigh.com

What's new?

  • Classes shifted to zoom

    The school had online classes on Teams since month of May last year, but due to Teams being a platform in which it is only possible to see few students at once, Zoom is being used for the classes now instead, with less technical glitches too.
